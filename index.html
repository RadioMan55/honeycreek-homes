Quality Comes First

Based out of Brookfield, WI HoneyCreek Homes, LLC founded by Todd and Kim Prudlow offers top quality at affordable rates for all of Southeastern WI. Our focus is to balance affordability and the customer’s needs.

We know that building a new home or remodeling your existing home is a big step and an even larger investment. Building with HoneyCreek Homes assures you that your dream is in the hands of a quality, experienced and honest builder.

    Our Company

    After growing up in the Wauwatosa and Milwaukee areas, Todd and Kim Prudlow and their 4 children moved to Brookfield in 2001.

    In 1995 Todd earned a degree from Marquette University in Mechanical Engineering. Todd always had a love of the home building process after helping his father and brothers with projects around his parent’s home and working construction jobs while in school on summer breaks. After a short time working as an Engineer, he followed his desire to be in the construction field. This lead to several years of working for custom home builders both in Michigan and Wisconsin before successfully striking out on his own. While he appreciated the knowledge he gained from his previous work, his focus on detail and the desire to provide customers a better built home at a fair and honest price led him to launch HoneyCreek Homes in 2003. HoneyCreek Homes strives to provide this by offering an “open book” approach to new home construction and remodeling.

    What does “Open Book” mean?:

    Unlike most other builders, HoneyCreek Homes opens its books to all customers so that they see exactly what they are spending their money on. A copy of each subcontractor’s invoice is provided with every billing packet as the project progresses. HoneyCreek Homes charges a negotiated upfront fee to cover their warranty, insurance, gas mileage, profit and overhead. Most other builders will charge all of these at additional rates. We strongly believe in an honest building process that leads to a lasting relationship with each customer.
